Ducati XDiavel Highlights

The 2021 Ducati XDiavel has been launched in India. The base Dark variant retails at Rs 18 lakh, whereas the higher-end Black Star costs Rs 22.60 lakh (both ex-showroom India). The latter features a more powerful Brembo M50 radial calipers up front, 2kg lighter wheels, premium cosmetic parts, and an exclusive livery. Take a look at the launch report for all the details.
